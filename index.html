My History

My journey began in 2014, at the age of 15, when I stood out among more than 400 candidates in a selection process for the machining mechanics course at Honda Autom√≥veis, being chosen as one of the 30 youngest apprentices. Over the course of two years, I acquired significant skills in programming industrial CNC machines and working on metric projects.

Simultaneously, I pursued a four-year high school/technical degree in computer science (three years of high school and one year of technical training). However, after completing my high school/technical education in 2017, I decided it wasn't the right time to pursue a career as a developer. I opted instead to graduate in Human Resources at college due to my affinity for managing people.

For two years, I worked in the Human Resources field in small and medium-sized companies, gaining valuable experience. Additionally, I had a brief but impactful experience as a Business Manager at Santander, which provided me with valuable insights into project management and stimulated my creative mindset.

Now, I am returning to my original passion: the field of development and design. I have always been fascinated by technology and the art of creativity through coding and design. This is where I believe my true calling lies. Furthermore, in the year 2023, I embarked on my college journey in Analysis and Development of Systems, aiming to further enhance my skills in this ever-evolving field.

Work

I have worked at renowned companies such as Honda Automobiles, FedEx, Burger King, and Santander, with over 8 years of experience in the market. I hold a degree in Human Resources and am currently pursuing a degree in Analysis and Systems Development with a focus on design and development.

I am in the process of learning, continuously expanding my knowledge in languages such as JavaScript, Python, Adobe and Figma tools.
